Dynamics Days US 2025

Please join us for the 43nd annual Dynamics Days US conference, to be held in Denver, January 3-5, 2025.

We welcome submissions of abstracts for contributed talks and poster presentations. We anticipate offering travel awards to students, postdocs, and other individuals without other sources of support.

We look forward to seeing you in Denver!

Statement on Diversity, Equity, and Inclusion*

We are committed to the goal of realizing an equitable, diverse, and inclusive professional community. To that end, we strive to uphold a conference environment that encourages the open expression and exchange of ideas; that is free from all forms of discrimination, harassment, and retaliation; and that is welcoming to all participants.

All conference participants are expected to work towards this commitment to equity, diversity, and inclusion, by helping to ensure that equitable opportunity and treatment are afforded to all participants regardless of gender, gender identity or expression, sexual orientation, race, color, national or ethnic origin, religion or religious belief, age, marital status, disabilities, veteran status, and field of expertise.
