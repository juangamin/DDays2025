Travel Awards

We will offer travel awards of at least $500 and up to $700. To be eligible for a travel award you must (i) submit an abstract and present your work as a talk or poster, (ii) register for the conference, and (iii) be based at a US institution (this is a requirement of our funding sources). If we receive more applications than we can support, we will give priority to students and postdocs.

Please apply for a travel award when submitting your abstract Here. Decisions on travel support will be emailed at the end of November.
