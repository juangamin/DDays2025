Reimbursement Process

If you are an invited speaker or received a Travel Award, follow these steps to process your reimbursement

Save your airplane ticket, hotel, transportation, and registration fee receipts. Also save itemized meal receipts (showing each ordered item, not just the credit card slip)
Scan and send these receipts to am_accnt@colorado.edu .
In this email, include your full name, the address where you want us to send your check, and whether or not you are a US citizen or green card holder.
Sign and email the documents that we will email back to you.
We will send you a check for your expenses, with the maximum amount being that detailed in your Travel Award email (this doesan't apply to invited speakers). If you are a local student, you will not be reimbursed for lodging or transportation costs.

Contact: dynamicsdays2025@gmail.com
