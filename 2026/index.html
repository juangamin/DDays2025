    Dynamics Days US

    Join us for the 44th Dynamics Days US workshop January 9-11, 2026! Dynamics Days US 2026 is hosted by the Department of Mathematics at the University of Arizona (UA) and the NSF Research Training Group in Data Driven Discovery, and will take place on UA's main campus in Tucson, Arizona.

    We got a late start this year but hope that despite this delay, we will see you in Tucson in January. More information coming very soon!

    Concurrent Workshop on Horse-Human Interactions

    Concurrent with Dynamics Days US 2026 is a workshop on Horse-Human Partnerships and Human-Agent Teams. This meeting will take place on the University of Arizona campus — one building away from Dynamics Days — with additional activities at the University's Al Marah Equine Center (AMEC). The workshop features topics of potential interest to Dynamics Days participants, including nonlinear dynamics and coupled oscillators, horse-rider synchrony, and AI and human-agent teams. Everyone attending Dynamics Days is welcome to attend talks in this workshop (and vice versa) without additional registration. Activities at AMEC may require an additional fee; see the workshop website for more details.

    Traveling to Tucson

    (updated January 6, 2026)

    The meeting space is in othe Environment and Natural Resources 2 (ENR2) Building on the main campus of the University of Arizona (UA). Look for Room N120 on the ground floor. The nearest parking structure is the Sixth Street Garage; the ENR2 building is adjacent and to the west of the garage. Below is a map of the area; click for larger version. See also walking directions from the hotel to the lecture hall.

    Visitors flying to Tucson can use either Tucson International Airport (TUS) or Phoenix Sky Harbor Airport (PHX). Note flights are sometimes significantly cheaper to PHX than to TUS. From PHX, Groome Transportation runs a shuttle directly to the ENR2 building where the workshop will be held (use the "University" stop). The conference hotel is a short walk away.
