Dynamics Days US

Join us for the 44th Dynamics Days US workshop January 9-11, 2026! Dynamics Days US 2026 is hosted by the Department of Mathematics at the University of Arizona (UA) and will take place on UA's main campus in Tucson, Arizona.

We got a late start this year but hope that despite this delay, we will see you in Tucson in January. More information coming very soon!

Tentative agenda

Talks will start first thing in the morning on Friday January 9, and continue through (early or mid) afternoon on Sunday January 11. There will be an evening reception on Saturday January 10.

Funding

We anticipate the availability of limited funding, especially for students, postdocs, and other junior participants. Also, please contact us if you need funding for childcare during the conference.

Registration

We are hammering out a couple final logistical details and will open registration soon. In the mean time, you can add your name to the Dynamics Days mailing list. This is a low-traffic list, and if you've attended or received news about Dynamics Days in the past, you should already be on it.

Location and travel

The meeting space is in the Environment and Natural Resources 2 (ENR2) Building on the main campus of the University of Arizona (UA). The nearest parking structure is the Sixth Street Garage; the ENR2 building is adjacent and to the west of the garage.

Visitors flying to Tucson can use either Tucson International Airport (TUS) or Phoenix Sky Harbor Airport (PHX). Flights are sometimes significantly cheaper to PHX than to TUS. If you fly to PHX, Groome Transportation runs a shuttle from PHX directly to the ENR2 building where the workshop will be held (use the "University" stop). The conference hotel is a short walk away.

We have reserved a block of rooms at Tucson Marriott University Park. To receive the conference rate of $164 / night, reserve a room by December 12, 2025.
